The Create Puna

The Create Puna is centered around
How might we as a group of creatives give back to our community through making, creating and sharing our skills?



The answer is:
- Creative workshops
- Woodwork, art, pottery, craft, sewing
- Producing sustainable products
- Creating product to sell
- Koha proceeds back to a creative space in the community
